Questions

Frequently asked

Is it really free to start?

Yes — 60 days on Starter, no credit card required. After 60 days you'll need an active membership to keep your center live. Reach out to sales any time before then to pick a plan, or keep using Starter features free while you decide.

Can I switch plans later?

Any time. Upgrade or downgrade in one click. If you downgrade to a plan with tighter limits (e.g. fewer classrooms), you'll be asked to archive data before the change completes — we never delete your records without confirmation.

How is pricing per daycare?

Each plan covers one daycare centre with unlimited parents. Run more than one centre? Premium's multi-daycare per owner covers you, and operators of 3+ centres qualify for volume pricing — contact sales.

Where is my data stored?

Firebase (Google Cloud) in North American regions. Your data is isolated per centre by Firestore security rules, encrypted at rest, and we're building toward PIPEDA-aligned controls. Export anytime.

Do you charge per parent or per child?

No. Parents are always free. Child headcount affects your plan's child limit — but within your limit, every parent, every child, every message is included.

What's the difference between Pro and Premium?

Pro is everything an operating daycare needs to run day-to-day — reports, staff timesheets, planners, branding. Premium adds multi-daycare management, our AI assistant, and API access for centres running custom integrations.
