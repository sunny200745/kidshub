Everything you need

One platform. Every task covered.

From morning check-in to end-of-day reports, KidsHub handles the admin so you can focus on the children.

📋

Digital Daily Logs

Log meals, naps, diaper changes, mood, and activities in seconds. Parents see updates in real-time through their app.

✅

Tap-to-Check Attendance

One-tap check-in and check-out with photo or PIN verification. Know exactly who is at your centre at any moment, with a full audit trail.

💬

Parent Messaging

Send updates, photos, and announcements directly to parents. Two-way messaging keeps families connected without constant phone calls.

💳

Billing & Invoicing

Generate invoices, track payments, and send automated reminders. Custom pricing per agency — no surprise per-child fees as you grow.

📊

Reports & Insights

Attendance trends, occupancy rates, and child snapshots — all exportable for licensing, subsidy claims, or your own planning.

📵

Offline Mode

Works without internet. iOS and Android apps continue logging data offline and sync automatically when connection is restored.
