# KidsHub — One-Pager

**The daycare OS parents actually want to open.**

Built by a daycare parent for daycare owners. One app replaces the
paper sheets, group texts, and printed schedules that clog a daycare's
day. Teachers log once, parents see it live, owners see the whole centre.

---

## What it is

KidsHub is a 3-in-1 platform:

| Audience | What they get |
| --- | --- |
| **Parents** | A mobile app with a live feed of their child's day — meals, naps, activities, photos, messages from the teacher, and today's schedule. |
| **Teachers** | A fast tablet/phone app to log everything in 2 taps. No more paper binders, no more personal phones for parent texts. |
| **Owners** | A web dashboard for attendance, enrollment, staff invites, announcements, and Pro reports (photos, health, billing). |
